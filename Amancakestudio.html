About Us

Our Story

Aman Cake Studio specializes in custom designer cakes, cupcakes, and baked treats that transform your celebrations into unforgettable experiences. Founded in 2021, our passion for baking and artistic design has made us a beloved bakery in Ludhiana.

Every cake we create is a masterpiece, crafted with the finest ingredients and attention to detail. Whether it's a birthday, wedding, or corporate event, we work closely with you to bring your vision to life.

Known for our quality and creativity, we take pride in making desserts that are as beautiful as they are delicious.
